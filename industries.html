Core System

Furniture & Lifestyle Commerce

Furniture is where supply chain theory meets physical reality.

Large volume, high return rates, oversized dimensions, and direct-to-consumer exposure make furniture one of the most failure-sensitive product categories. YiShen engineers furniture not as products, but as logistics-resilient systems.

  • Structural & Fatigue Validation (BIFMA / ANSI)
  • ISTA 6A Packaging Engineering for parcel & container stress
  • DDP Oversize Networks across US, EU & GCC
  • SKU-Level Survivability Modeling
