Core Industries We Serve

Each industry below represents a distinct procurement logic, compliance framework, packaging strategy, and delivery rhythm.

Commercial Office

Ergonomic chairs, workstations, conference systems, and large-volume office deployment.

  • BIFMA / EN1335 compliance
  • Project-based MOQs
  • Phased delivery planning

Home & Residential

Home office seating, desks, and lifestyle furniture optimized for eCommerce and retail programs.

  • KD flat-pack optimization
  • Drop-test packaging logic
  • Retail-ready cartons

Gaming & Lifestyle

Gaming chairs and performance seating for DTC brands and global distributors.

  • Brand differentiation support
  • High-density container loading
  • Fast SKU iteration

Hospitality

Seating and furniture systems for hotels, serviced apartments, and public spaces.

  • Durability-focused materials
  • Fire & safety considerations
  • Batch project execution

Education

Classroom, training, and institutional furniture designed for long-term use and cost control.

  • Standardized configurations
  • Budget-sensitive design
  • Long lifecycle planning

Industrial & Projects

Non-standard furniture, OEM components, and mixed-category supply chain projects.

  • OEM / ODM execution
  • Custom packaging & logistics
  • Cross-category coordination
