Foundation Logic

Why Furniture Is the Hardest Test Case

Furniture is one of the most unforgiving product categories in global trade. If a system can control furniture risk, it can be extended to other industries with confidence.

  • Oversized dimensions & high cubic cost
  • High return sensitivity (especially DTC)
  • Mixed material structures
  • Direct consumer & brand exposure

Engineering Result

Furniture becomes the training ground for:

  • Packaging survivability modeling
  • SKU-level logistics optimization
  • Cost predictability under scale
  • Compliance without over-engineering
