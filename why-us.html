The YiShen Furniture Engineering Lab

We do not ship furniture.
We engineer survivable furniture systems.

Every chair, desk, and furniture SKU is treated as a structural asset. Before entering global circulation, each SKU is validated for load integrity, packaging survivability, and logistics stress.

  • BIFMA / ANSI Structural Validation — fatigue, load & stability
  • ISTA 6A Packaging Engineering — drop, vibration, compression
  • Material Traceability — mesh, steel, mechanisms, gas lifts
  • DDP Survivability Modeling — parcel + container stress
