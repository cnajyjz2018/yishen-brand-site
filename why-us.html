Inside the Lab

The YiShen Engineering Lab

Most logistics providers react to problems. We eliminate them upstream.

YiShen Global operates a vertically integrated engineering system that treats manufacturing, packaging, and logistics as a single controllable equation. Each SKU is validated for structural integrity, packaging resilience, and logistics survivability before it ever enters global circulation.

  • Structural Integrity Validation: BIFMA / ANSI fatigue and load testing at component level.
  • ISTA 6A Packaging Engineering: Drop, vibration, and compression testing for DTC survivability.
  • Material Traceability: Controlled sourcing of mesh, steel, plastics, and gas lifts.
  • Claim Prevention Focus: Data-driven reduction of post-sale failures and returns.
