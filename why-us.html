Most suppliers focus on products. YiShen Global focuses on **survivability, predictability, and control** across the entire furniture lifecycle — from structure and packaging to global delivery.

Core Philosophy

Furniture Is Not a Product. It Is a System.

Furniture fails globally not because of design — but because it is treated as an isolated product instead of a **load-bearing, compliance-bound, logistics-exposed system**.

❌ The Industry Default

Design → Produce → Ship → Hope. Packaging, compliance, and delivery risks are discovered only after failure.

✔ The YiShen Model

Engineer → Validate → Simulate → Control. Failure points are eliminated before market entry.

✔ The Result

Lower damage rates, stable lead times, predictable landed cost, and long-term buyer confidence.

Inside the Lab

The YiShen Furniture Engineering Lab

We do not test products for marketing. We test systems for survival.

Every SKU entering our system is treated as an industrial asset. Structural load, fatigue, packaging survivability, and logistics stress are validated before volume production.

  • BIFMA / ANSI Structural Validation — fatigue, load & stability
  • ISTA 6A Packaging Engineering — drop, vibration, compression
  • Material Traceability — mesh, steel, mechanisms, gas lifts
  • DDP Survivability Modeling — parcel & container stress

What This Means for Buyers
