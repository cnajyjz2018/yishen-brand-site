YiShen Global is an engineering-led furniture supply chain system, designed to reduce uncertainty, not just move products.

1. The Real Problem We Solve

Most failures in global furniture sourcing are not caused by product quality.

They are caused by system instability: inconsistent lead time, unclear responsibility, packaging failures, compliance surprises, and poor communication between factory, logistics, and buyer.

YiShen Global exists to engineer stability into this system.

2. Our Engineering Principles

  • Furniture-first design
    Every decision starts from furniture structure, materials, packaging survivability, and end-use environment.
  • Process before price
    Price without process control only creates downstream risk.
  • Responsibility clarity
    Each step has an owner: design, production, packing, loading, clearance, delivery.
  • Predictability over promises
    We engineer lead time, not explain delays.
