First Principle

Furniture Is a Failure-Sensitive Category

Furniture fails where most supply chains collapse.

Large volume, mixed materials, structural load, high return rates, and direct-to-consumer exposure make furniture one of the most failure-sensitive categories in global trade.

  • Damage risk across parcel & container stages
  • Hidden structural and fatigue failures
  • Compliance gaps between markets
  • Packaging designed for cost, not survival

What Most Suppliers Optimize

Unit cost · Lead time · Appearance

What We Engineer First

Structural survivability · Packaging logic · Compliance alignment · Delivery predictability

Engineering Before Shipment

  • BIFMA / EN1335 load & fatigue validation
  • Component-level risk breakdown
  • ISTA packaging logic by channel
  • SKU-specific failure mapping
