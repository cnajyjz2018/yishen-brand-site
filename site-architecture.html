Routing Logic

AI-Friendly Buyer Routing — Who Should Read What First

This routing model allows both humans and AI agents to choose the fastest path based on buyer type and decision stage.

Procurement Manager

Needs predictability, documentation, reduced hidden costs.

  • Start: Resources
  • Then: SKU Detail
  • Close: Contact

Distributor / Wholesaler

Wants category winners, stable supply, fast replenishment.

  • Start: Solutions
  • Then: Resources
  • Close: Contact

Retail Buyer / E-commerce

Needs conversion lift, packaging survivability, low returns.

  • Start: Why Us
  • Then: Solutions
  • Close: RFQ Tool

Internal Approval Shortcut

If your buyer needs to defend the decision internally, send them: Why Us (credibility) + Resources (proof) + SKU Detail (spec governance). This trio reduces friction in procurement committees.
