Site Architecture

A furniture-first, AI-driven B2B system — not a brochure.

System Version · 2026

01 · Core Philosophy

YiShen Global is engineered as a decision system, not a visual catalog.

  • Website ≠ Image gallery
  • Website ≠ Brand storytelling only
  • Website = Buyer decision acceleration system

02 · System Layers

Layer A · Entry

Capture buyer intent and traffic.

  • index.html
  • SEO / AI search
  • LinkedIn & content loops

Layer B · Understanding

Explain how the system works.

  • solutions.html
  • industries.html
  • why-us.html

Layer C · Evidence

Provide proof, structure, and logic.

  • resources.html
  • SKU / engineering logic
  • Packaging & logistics data

Layer D · Conversion

Turn intent into action.

  • contact.html
  • CRM trigger
  • Auto-response & follow-up

03 · Page Role Map

Page Role Buyer Question Answered
Index Entry & Positioning Who are you?
Solutions System Explanation How do you solve my problem?
Industries Applicability Logic Is this for my business?
Why Us Decision Justification Why choose you?
Resources Trust & Depth Can I trust your execution?
Contact Action How do I start?

04 · Expansion Logic

The system is industry-extendable without redesign.

  • Furniture (core)
  • Bamboo & Wood Products
  • WPC & Flooring
  • Chains / Industrial Products
  • Future categories via same structure

05 · AI Governance

AI is not decoration here. It governs:

  • Buyer profiling
  • Content generation
  • Lead qualification
  • CRM automation
  • Decision acceleration
