The Problem We Solve

Most furniture supply chains fail not because of quality, but because of unpredictability.

  • Unclear structural feasibility
  • Packaging damage after production
  • Logistics cost volatility
  • Execution risk shifting between parties

YiShen exists to remove these uncertainties before they become losses.

Our System Architecture

Every project follows the same engineering decision structure.

  • Engineering Review – structural logic, materials, tolerances
  • Packaging & SOP Design – drop risk, carton logic, pallet rules
  • Cost Predictability – BOM stability, logistics volatility
  • Logistics Architecture – FOB / CIF / DDP by market
  • Execution Control – inspection, loading, delivery SOP

No quotation before engineering clarity. No execution without predictability.
