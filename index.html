Frequently Asked Questions

What is your MOQ?

For standard models, our MOQ starts from 50pcs. For OEM customization, it typically starts from 200pcs.

What are your payment terms?

We usually accept T/T (30% deposit, 70% against BL copy) or L/C at sight for large orders.

Do you offer samples?

Yes, samples can be shipped via air express (FedEx/DHL) within 7 days for your evaluation.

What is the warranty?

We offer a 3-5 year warranty on core components (gas lift, mechanism) and 2 years on upholstery.
