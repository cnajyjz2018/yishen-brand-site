Why Furniture Requires a Different System

Furniture fails globally not because of product design — but because of **packaging collapse, compliance mismatch, container inefficiency and last-mile unpredictability**.

YiShen Global engineers furniture as a **logistics-resilient system** before it ever reaches the market.

Primary System

DTC & Retail Furniture Fulfillment

Direct-to-consumer furniture magnifies every weakness.

Oversized parcels, high return exposure, and consumer-visible damage risk make DTC furniture one of the most failure-sensitive categories in global trade.

  • ISTA 6A + real-world drop-path packaging engineering
  • KD structure optimization for chairs, desks & boxed furniture
  • DDP oversize delivery networks (US / EU / GCC)
  • Damage-rate & return-risk control logic

Typical Result: Damage rate reduced from 6–9% → below 1.5%.

Designed For

E-commerce brands, Amazon sellers, omnichannel retailers and DTC programs scaling beyond trial volumes.
