Furniture Supply Chain Solutions

Not isolated products — but a complete system connecting furniture manufacturing, packaging and delivery.

The Problem We Solve

Most furniture sourcing fails not because of product quality, but because manufacturing, packaging and logistics are treated as separate decisions.

YiShen Global integrates these layers into one system, reducing uncertainty before pricing discussions begin.

Our Solution Philosophy

We engineer furniture supply chains as predictable systems. Every SKU is designed with:

  • Manufacturing stability
  • Packaging survivability
  • Container efficiency
  • Market-specific compliance

Core Solution Modules

Manufacturing Integration

Furniture production aligned with export markets, including office seating, home furniture and outdoor categories.

Materials include metal, wood, bamboo, WPC and hybrid structures.

Packaging Engineering

KD, compressed and modular packaging designed to survive international transport and reduce landed cost.

Logistics & Delivery

FOB, CIF and DDP models structured around furniture dimensions, weight distribution and destination regulations.

Risk & Compliance Control

Compliance planning for BIFMA, EN, fire standards, material regulations and local market requirements.

Who These Solutions Are For

Furniture Brands & Importers

Brands seeking stable SKUs, predictable delivery and scalable supply chain partners.

Distributors & Project Buyers

Buyers managing office, residential or hospitality projects requiring coordination across multiple furniture categories.
