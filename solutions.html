How YiShen Solutions Work

Every solution we provide is built on the same core logic: transform fragmented furniture sourcing into a structured, scalable, and controllable system.

  • SKU-level engineering, not catalog selling
  • Compliance designed upfront, not patched later
  • Packaging & logistics engineered as part of the product
  • Delivery predictability prioritized over lowest price

1. SKU Architecture

We define each product as a structured SKU unit with clear specifications, variants, and scalability logic.

  • SKU family & variant mapping
  • Target market positioning
  • MOQ & lead-time modeling
  • Upgrade / downgrade pathways

2. Engineering & Compliance

Compliance is not paperwork. It is an engineering decision.

  • BIFMA / EN / ISO / market-specific standards
  • Load, durability & lifecycle assumptions
  • Material & component traceability
  • Risk points flagged before production

3. Packaging Engineering

Packaging is treated as a mechanical system, not decoration.

  • KD vs assembled decision logic
  • Drop-test & compression assumptions
  • Container density optimization
  • E-commerce vs retail packaging split

4. Delivery Systems

Delivery models are designed to match buyer capability, not supplier convenience.

  • FOB / CIF / DDP comparison
  • Regional warehouse support
  • Timeline visibility & milestone control
  • After-sales risk isolation

Who These Solutions Are Built For

  • Furniture brands building scalable product lines
  • Distributors managing multi-country compliance
  • Retailers needing predictable delivery windows
  • E-commerce sellers optimizing packaging & returns
  • Project buyers requiring documentation & control
