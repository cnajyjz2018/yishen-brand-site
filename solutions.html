Why Traditional Sourcing Fails

Most furniture sourcing problems are not caused by factories, materials, or even pricing.

They fail because the system is fragmented: product decisions, packaging, compliance, manufacturing, and delivery are handled independently.

YiShen Global solves this by engineering these elements into one controllable framework.

Our Solution Modules

Product Structure Engineering

We define SKU architecture, component logic, material selection, and tolerance standards before production begins.

This prevents downstream instability and uncontrolled variation across factories.

Packaging & Load Optimization

KD logic, carton structure, drop-risk mitigation, and container density are engineered as part of the product.

This directly impacts landed cost, damage rate, and e-commerce viability.

Compliance Planning

BIFMA, EN1335, CE, UL and market-specific requirements are integrated early in the design stage.

Compliance is treated as a pathway, not an emergency correction.

Manufacturing Coordination

We align the right factories by category, complexity, and risk profile.

This avoids overloading single suppliers and reduces dependency risk.

Delivery Model Design

FOB, CIF, or DDP models are selected based on buyer structure, destination, and internal accountability.

Responsibility is clearly defined across the delivery chain.

Program Ownership

YiShen Global operates as a single accountable system, not a collection of disconnected vendors.

Buyers gain clarity, control, and repeatability.
