Engineering Execution Flow

Every project follows a fixed engineering decision path. No quotation before validation. No execution without predictability.

01 · Inquiry Intake

Product intent, target market, volume expectation, and risk boundaries are clarified before any technical discussion begins.

02 · Engineering Review

Structural logic, materials, tolerance stacking, and assembly feasibility are validated before quotation.

03 · Packaging & SOP Design

Drop-risk logic, carton structure, pallet rules, and warehouse SOP are engineered as part of the product system.

04 · Cost Predictability Modeling

BOM stability, logistics volatility, and currency exposure are modeled at SKU level to avoid future surprises.

05 · Logistics Architecture

FOB / CIF / DDP routing is engineered per market based on compliance, lead-time, and risk ownership.

06 · Execution & Delivery

Production, inspection, loading, and delivery follow predefined SOP with timeline transparency.
