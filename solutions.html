Core Solutions

DDP Air for Office & Gaming Chairs

Ideal for high-value, mid-volume chair SKUs. We combine DDP air and express routes for USA, EU and GCC with carton optimization and compliance support.

Best for: New chair launches, urgent replenishment, high-margin SKUs.

DDP Sea for Bulk Furniture

Cost-optimized DDP sea freight for office chairs, sofas and home furniture, with full container loading plans and pallet strategies.

Best for: Mature products, stable replenishment cycles, container-scale orders.

Hybrid Sea + Air Programs

We design hybrid models: base volume by sea, “top-up” and urgent SKUs by air or express to avoid stock-outs.

Best for: Brands and importers balancing cost and speed.
