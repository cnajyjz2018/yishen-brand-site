Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from B2B buyers regarding our operations.

Do you handle DDP shipping to the US?

Yes. We offer full DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) services, managing customs, duties, and inland freight directly to your warehouse.

What is your standard MOQ?

For standardized SKUs, our MOQ is flexible (typically 50-100 units). For custom OEM projects, it starts at 1x 20GP container.

Are your products BIFMA/UL certified?

Yes. Our office furniture is tested to BIFMA X5.1 standards, and our electronic components carry UL/ETL certifications.

Can you provide private labeling?

Absolutely. We offer full white-label services, including custom box art, user manuals, and product branding.
