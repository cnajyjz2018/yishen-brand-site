Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from B2B buyers regarding our operations.

Do you handle DDP shipping?

Yes. We offer full DDP services, managing customs, duties, and inland freight directly to your warehouse.

What is your standard MOQ?

Are products BIFMA/UL certified?

Yes. Furniture is BIFMA X5.1 tested; electronic components carry UL/ETL certifications.

Absolutely. We offer full white-label services, including custom box art, manuals, and branding.
